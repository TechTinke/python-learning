KU hostel residents reporting maintenance issues (broken tap, no power, etc.) to the warden.

Logged-in students submit a complaint (room number, category, description).
Each complaint has a status field (Pending/Resolved) that only staff (is_staff users) can change.
Students should NOT be able to see other students' complaints, only their own — list view must filter by request.user.

Filtering querysets by the logged-in user (Complaint.objects.filter(owner=request.user)),
role-based permission checks (request.user.is_staff), model with ForeignKey to User.
